# C4 — Cross-Domain Events and Data Flows (cross-phase contract)

**Status: DRAFT v0.1 — frozen (v1) at foundation exit; thereafter versioned only by Main Architect.**
**Purpose:** the seams BETWEEN parallel phases — each entry names producer, consumer, and the invariant the Tier-2 gate checks. Sources are ERP-start repo-root-relative under `docs/Business/`. Pin: ERP-start@51e32b0.

| # | Event / flow | Producer (phase) | Consumer (phase) | Frozen invariant |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| E1 | Salary eligibility | service-orders (ZN status engine; sale-doc posting for no-ZN conts) | payroll | eligibility date = EARLIEST of ZN `ready_for_release`/`released`; restant-origin ZN re-enters on its own dates; unexecuted restant works never accrue; upsale conts tip `vanzare_piese` (no ZN) accrue at sale-document posting |
| E2 | Vehicle release | service-orders | treasury (settlement), reporting | release procedure → sale document (per cont) + act predare in ONE tx; debt = full cont total; revenue split per service-orders.md formula into R18 |
| E3 | Payment allocation | treasury | service-orders (paid-state), payroll (salary payouts), reporting | a payment carries 1..n allocation portions, each → exactly one cont; unallocated remainder = avans neutilizat; reference-bonus accrual fires pro-rata per allocated portion, capped per contributor |
| E4 | VAT-attribute cascade | inventory (supplier FF linkage) | reporting (sinecost), service-orders (cont margins) | FF linkage flips VAT attribute on purchase docs → stock cost layers → sinecost recalc on affected conts, atomically; the ONLY upstream cascade (ADR-005 R3) |
| E5 | Stock release at delivery | service-orders (sale document) | inventory (lot truth) | releases EXACTLY the lots `in_producere` for the delivered ZNs (no picking at delivery); `vanzare_piese` = direct on_hand→consumed; undelivered → `incomplete_delivery_negative` + restant ZN migration |
| E6 | Fiscal-number lifecycle | foundation (machinery) | inventory (15A producer; 15B for retur-client), service-orders (15B vânzare) | diapazon: auto-next-but-editable, release-on-cancel; e-factura: imported number, remove-on-cancel; cancelled invoice = zero impact + R9B storno; invoice total ≡ linked cont total (save-time validation); 15A's linked-document set extends to treasury's M1 service acquisitions (ADR-006 §Service acquisition: R9B at fiscal-invoice linkage — refine the 15A dimension at foundation freeze) |
| E7 | Defectare conversion | service-orders | treasury (relink), payroll (intact salary) | within `defectare_conversion_window` from assessment completion: sale storno (R9B only via accounting blocking ticket) + payment reclassification to main cont + ZN migration with salary INTACT; beyond window: non-refundable |
| E8 | Ticket raising | any | any (addressee role) | all five ticket types go through the C3 unified entity; blocking tickets stop their flow until resolved |
| E9 | Painter-material debt | inventory-procurement (painter issue) | payroll (payout deduction) | painter issue posts R7 `painter_material_debt` at (1 − `painter_material_company_share`) of cost; payroll deducts it at payout alongside tool installments + overpayment withholdings; atomic R2+R4+R7+R8 at issue (effect-matrix item 6) |
| E10 | ZN-line quantity split | service-orders (sole owner of the split action) | inventory (reservation truth) | split atomically creates independent ZN lines AND redistributes existing reservations to the resulting lines (effect-matrix item 20); reservation linkage survives at ZN-line granularity |
| E11 | Procurement readiness auto-update | inventory-procurement (reception confirms all parts) | service-orders (ZN Layer-4 status) | when the warehouse confirms all required parts received, ZN procurement-readiness may flip to `all_required_parts_ready` automatically; `critical_parts_ready` stays a manual decision (Gheorghe/Grigore right) |
| E12 | Outsource confirmation | service-orders (ZN outsource etapa) | treasury-payments (documents) | confirming outsource work on a ZN auto-creates the service-acquisition (+ payment form) for the outsourcer — ZN-linked, feeds sinecost, standard supplier flow, NOT under the M1 confidential scope |
